Doctor Appointment Booking Form

Description:-

This webpage features a doctor appointment form designed to schedule medical visits. It collects essential details such as appointment date and time, patient name, gender, contact number, and address. The form also includes a section for user queries and allows patients to select different types of medical checkups. It demonstrates the use of various HTML elements like text inputs, date and time pickers, radio buttons, checkboxes, and textarea for an organized booking experience.
